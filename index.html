Hi, I'm Maia! I'm currently an undergraduate at the University of Michigan, and am an incoming intern at Relativity Space for HITL Avionics Testing! The type of work I enjoy most is team-oriented, technically challenging, hands-on, and varied—meaning I'd be working with others and tackling new challenges frequently.

My current areas of interest are:
  • Embedded Systems
  • Robotics
  • Computer security
  • Space systems
So, why space? The space industry lies at the confluence of my two driving goals: solving complex technical problems and raising the overall quality of life for humanity. Space abounds with resources that can better life on Earth, both as a domain we can leverage and for the physical materials and land we stand to gain. (Also, space is stunning. It's impossible not to be utterly entranced.)

When I’m not building something new, you can find me cooking, gardening, boxing, or spending time with friends.
